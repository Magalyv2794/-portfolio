Mathematics Instructor

As a Mathematics Instructor at the Technological University of Panama, I contribute in the academic development of first-year students during mandatory summer courses from January to April 2021. My responsibilities extended beyond conventional teaching to encompass mentorship and skill-building, ensuring students from diverse backgrounds were equipped with the essential mathematical foundation for their university journey.

  • Primary Mathematics Tutoring: Delivered approximately 150 hours of instruction, sticking to the prescribed manual of basic mathemathics skills established by the university.

  • Customized Instruction: Employed interactive teaching strategies to engage students.

  • Academic Reinforcement: Reinforced core mathematical concepts to bridge gaps in understanding and facilitated interactive sessions aimed at consolidationg prior knowledge.

  • Student Support: Provided personalized guidance and mentorship to empower students effective study techniques and problem-solving skills.
