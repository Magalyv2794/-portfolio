Civil Engineer Assistant

At Petroleum Derivates Company, I contribute to the transformative Porta Norte Project in 2021. This project aimed to develop a cityscape that integrates nature with expansive public spaces,fostering community engagement and sustainable urban living.

As a member of the Porta Norte project's Phase 1, my responsibilities encompassed a diverse range of tasks:

  • Productivity Management: Maintained daily productivity levels, ensuring efficient progress.

  • Yield Calculation: Calculations to assess yield rates, optimizing resource allocation and enhacing operationla efficiency.

  • Financial Accountability: Preparing detailed reports for both internal stakeholders and external partners, ensuring financial transparency and accountability.

  • Quality Control: Led quality control efforts, developing and implementing field tests for concrete and cobbles, guaranteeing high standards of structural integrity and durability.
